Introduction

Welcome to my campus food blog, where I highlight some of the best places to grab a bite near the Petty building. Whether you’re craving coffee, pizza, or a fresh burrito bowl, there are plenty of great options just steps away. In this blog, I’ll share three of my favorite spots on Tate Street, along with my top dish recommendations from each one. My goal is to help fellow students and visitors discover tasty, convenient, and affordable food close to campus.

Tate Street Coffee House

Tate Street Coffee House is located at 334 Tate Street, Greensboro, NC 27403, open every day from 6:30 AM to 5:00 PM. It’s a cozy café that’s been serving Greensboro since 1993, offering organic coffee, signature lattes, ciders, and hot chocolate. They also serve breakfast items like bagels, pastries, and muffins, plus lunch options and decadent desserts. It’s a great spot near UNCG for early risers, afternoon pick-me-ups, or winding down with friends in the evening.

Special Offers & Notes

Students can use Flex Dollars at this café for any menu item. This week’s promotion: buy a latte and get a pastry at half price!

Signature Latte

A bold latte made with organic fair-trade beans—perfect for waking up.

Bagel with Cream Cheese

A classic breakfast option that pairs perfectly with their coffee.

Pastry of the Day

Usually a muffin or croissant—sweet, flaky, ideal with your morning brew.

Chipotle Mexican Grill

This Chipotle is at 435 Tate St, Greensboro, NC 27403, near the intersection with Spring Garden. Chipotle Locations The store is open daily from 10:45 AM to 11:00 PM for pickup and delivery. Chipotle Locations Their menu allows you to customize: pick your meat or sofritas, base (rice, beans, etc.), and tons of toppings (salsas, guac, cheese). Chipotle Locations It’s a go-to for fast, customizable Mexican food—great for hot burritos, easy bowls, or fresh lifestyle options.

Burrito Bowl

Choose your base, protein, toppings, and salsa for a personalized meal.

Lifestyle Bowl

For whatever you need—vegan, keto, or just wanting something lighter with style.

Chips & Guacamole

Crispy tortilla chips served with fresh, creamy guacamole—perfect side or snack.

Manhattan Pizza & Subs

Manhattan Pizza & Subs is at 449 Tate Street, Greensboro, NC 27403, and opens daily starting at 11:00 AM. It offers a huge variety: pizzas, hot and cold subs, pasta dishes, salads, and classic sides. They also have deals like “5% off orders over $20” and specials on combos of pasta and pizzas. Happy, casual environment—good for sharing with friends, grabbing a late-night slice, or a hearty dinner after classes.

Meat Lover's Pizza

Loaded with pepperoni, sausage, ham, and bacon—perfect for meat lovers.

Veggie Pizza

Mushrooms, peppers, onions, olives, fresh tomatoes—colorful and fresh.

Chef’s Salad

A hearty mix of greens, turkey, provolone & more—lighter but filling.

Conclusion

Whether you need a caffeine boost, a slice of pizza, or a customizable burrito bowl, these three spots on Tate Street have you covered. Each offers something unique, from the cozy atmosphere of Tate Street Coffee House to the hearty options at Manhattan Pizza & Subs and the fresh flavors at Chipotle Mexican Grill. I hope this guide helps you discover some delicious food options near the Petty building. Happy eating!
